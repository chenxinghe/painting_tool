Help

The @GoLoGoLo is a desktop Java application for creating and editing Logos.The Logo is presented in the work space.And users are allowed to add circle, rectangle,triangle and texts to the work space.Also, they are also allow to change the shape and color of the shapes.Eventually, they will get their own logo

Step 1: Adding shapes and text

To start, use can use the buttons to add shapes and text which are located at the upper right of the window.

Step 2: change shape

Select the item you want to change shape and it should show the vertex of the shape, and then user can drag the vertex to change shape.

Step 3: Change

Select the item you want to change color, and you can use the tool bar to change colors which is at the right side of the window.

Step 4: Delete Items

Should you wish to delete a item, select it from the workspace and then click on the Delete Selected Item button.

Step 5: Move Items Up and Down

By clicking on the Move Up and Move Down buttons you can move selected items in the list up and down the table.

Step 5: Save the works

Once you've completed your work, you can press the save button and give your file a name. .

Step 6: Export your Gologolo

Press the Export button when you wish to export your Gologolo to a Web page summarizing the Gologolo.
